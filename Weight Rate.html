This system is basically responsible for how your body burns a crap ton of short term cals or in other words body fat(the conversion is 3500 cals per lb).
In order to be able to lose weight, youll have to calculate your metabolism using this calculator: Calculate your metabolism here.

So now how do we lose weight?
Answer: GO ON A CALORIE DEFICIT.

According to a medical article," A calorie deficit occurs when the number of calories a person consumes in a day is smaller than the number of calories they burn."
In dummies terms it means eat less food(calories) than your normal metabolic rate or what you usually would eat. For example: if my metabolism is 2500(meaning i could eat about 3-4 moderate meals a day on avg).
Then I would reduce the number of calories Im eating below my metabolism of 2500.
Its crucial to set healthy goals and not starve yourself. For reference I would try to go below 500 cals from your metabolism by cutting high calorie foods. Thus, i will be in a caloric defficient state wherein I hopefully you too will lose weight.
